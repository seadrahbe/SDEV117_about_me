About Me
Seadrah Berger

Introduction

Hello, my name is Seadrah! Although I was born in Tacoma, Washington, I was raised all around the Pacific Northwest, spending around 7 years in Oregon and 7 years in Idaho during my childhood. I've moved around 50 times and have attended 13 different schools. From my time spent traveling state-to-state, I have acclimated to the unique culture of each state, and experienced the wide diversity of the families living within each state. In high school, I settled down in Washington and have stayed here ever since. I attend Green River College, where I've spent some time exploring classes and developing my passions. After a journey of majoring in Environmental Science, Communications, Software Development, and Computer Science (consecutively), I've settled into Software Development and decided to complete my studies at Green River while staying active in student life.

Interests

  • Communication - I majored in communication briefly, as it's something that has fascinated me since high school. In high school, I suffered From social anxiety, and struggled to speak to others. However, I spent a year studying, listening to podcasts, and building my Communication skills, which paid off spectacularly. I believe communication is one of the most beneficial skills to develop.
  • Social Justice - It's important for me to uphold ethical standards in the work I do in the technology sector, especially in the current political climate. I'm passionate about advocating for the human rights and representation of marginalized communities, especially through diversity, equity, and inclusion efforts.
  • Poetry - Poetry and art in general is one of my favorite outlets for creativity and self-expression. I think artistic expression is empowering and can help us express the parts of ourself that lack a voice.
